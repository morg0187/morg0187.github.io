About Us

Nyltiak Public Library is a single branch library located in the municipality of Nyltiak, about 45 minutes outside of Ottawa. We serve a community of just over 2000 people. We are dedicated to bringing big city quality of service to our small town library. Our top notch acquisitions team is always looking for the latest and greatest items to add to our small but mighty collection. You can see a list of our newest items here.
